
Version Space Learning

There have been some sick people lately and we think it may be the food so
we want to learn what might be the problem.

We can use use version-space learning on this problem.  Here are the input 
examples (yes under reaction indicates that the person got sick):

Number  Restaurant  Meal        Day        Cost        Reaction
-------------------------------------------------------------
1       Sam's       breakfast   Friday     cheap         yes
2       Lobdell     lunch       Friday     expensive     no
3       Sam's       lunch       Saturday   cheap         yes
4       Jill's      breakfast   Sunday     cheap         no
5       Sam's       breakfast   Sunday     expensive     no
